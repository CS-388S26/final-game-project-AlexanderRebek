GAME CONTROLS


PLACING A TURRET
----------------
1. Tap a turret button in the shop panel on the left
2. Tap again and while holding your finger down, drag to reposition the turret
3. Lift your finger to place the turret


SELLING A TURRET
----------------
- Double tap on a placed turret to sell it and recover its full cost


VIEWING A TURRET ATTACK RANGE
--------------------------------
- Press and hold on a placed turret for 1 second to show its range circle
- Lift your finger to hide it
- On supported devices, pressing harder (pressure detector) shows it immediately

ROUNDS
------
- At the start of each break you receive money for the upcoming round
- Use the break time to place or rearrange turrets before enemies appear


PLAYER HEALTH
-------------
- Every enemy that reaches the end of the path deals damage to the player
- Health does not reset between rounds
